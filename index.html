      Frequently Asked Questions

      What happens if I'm late? expand_more

      Session time starts at the booked hour. Being late will reduce your shooting time as we often have consecutive bookings.

      Can I have the RAW files? expand_more

      We do not deliver RAW or unedited files. Part of our studio's value is the curated editing and color grading process.

      Studio shoe policy? expand_more

      To keep our white cyclorama pristine, we ask that all shoes used for shooting have their soles taped or are thoroughly cleaned before stepping on the wall.
